About USvCN

USvCN is a meme-based cryptocurrency on the BNB Smart Chain that represents the ongoing geopolitical tension between the United States and China. Featuring iconic national symbols and cultural rivalry, USvCN is designed to be a fun, engaging, and community-driven token.
